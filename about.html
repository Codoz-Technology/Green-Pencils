About Us

Our team is passionate about crafting high-quality, eco-friendly pencils that inspire creativity while caring for the planet. Each pencil we make reflects our dedication to sustainability and craftsmanship.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

With over 12 years of expertise in paper pencil manufacturing, Greenmate specializes in producing high-quality, eco-friendly pencils made from recycled paper. Our commitment to sustainability and innovation has positioned us as a trusted name in the industry, serving educational institutions, businesses, and conscious consumers across the globe.

Greenmate is a trusted OEM supplier of eco-friendly pencils made from recycled paper and newspaper. We specialize in sustainable, high-quality solutions tailored for schools, brands, and corporate clients delivering reliable, customizable products backed by a strong commitment to environmental responsibility.
