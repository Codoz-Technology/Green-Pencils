Our Manufacturing Equipment

Watch our machinery in action and see how we create high-quality eco-friendly pencils

Rolling Machine

The rolling machine is a key component in the paper pencil manufacturing process. It precisely wraps recycled paper around a graphite lead, forming the pencil's core structure. Designed for speed, accuracy, and consistency, it ensures strong bonding and smooth finishing—ideal for high-quality, eco-friendly pencil production.

End Cutting Machine

The dual end cutting machine ensures that every paper pencil is trimmed to the exact same length from both ends. It provides precision cutting for a clean, professional finish—essential for maintaining consistent size, appearance, and quality in high-volume pencil production.

Polishing Machine

The polishing machine smoothens and refines the surface of paper pencils, enhancing their finish and appearance. It removes minor imperfections and prepares the pencils for coloring or packaging—ensuring a clean and high-quality final product or for further processing like branding or coloring that meets export-grade standards.

Pen Rolling Machine

This eco-friendly white pencil is crafted from upcycled industrial paper waste, offering a clean, modern look with zero wood usage. Durable, smooth, and sustainable, it's a smart alternative to traditional pencils—perfect for schools, offices, and eco-conscious brands. By using repurposed materials, it helps reduce landfill waste and supports a circular economy.
